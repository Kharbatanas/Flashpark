Motion & Animation Style

How should Flashpark move? Pick the animation philosophy.

A

Subtle & Refined

Micro-interactions only. Soft fade-ins on scroll (0.3s), gentle hover lifts on cards, smooth page transitions. Nothing distracting — motion serves usability. Think Apple.com.

fade-in on scroll hover lift smooth transitions skeleton loaders
B

Cinematic & Immersive

Hero parallax scrolling, staggered card reveals, number counters animating up, scroll-triggered sections that slide/scale in. Landing page feels like a story. Think Stripe.com or Linear.app.

parallax hero staggered reveals scroll animations number counters page transitions
C

Playful & Bouncy

Spring physics, bouncy card interactions, Lottie illustrations that react, drag gestures, confetti on booking confirmation. Fun and memorable. Think Notion or Figma.

spring physics bouncy cards Lottie reactions drag gestures confetti

🎯 Recommendation: For Premium + Warm Sand, option B (Cinematic) works best — it tells a story on the landing page while keeping the app itself clean. The parallax + staggered reveals feel luxury without being over the top.
