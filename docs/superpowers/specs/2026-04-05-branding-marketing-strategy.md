## 1. NAME ANALYSIS

### Current Name: Flashpark

| Criteria | Score | Notes |
|----------|-------|-------|
| Relevance to parking | 9/10 | "Park" is instantly clear |
| Memorability | 7/10 | Simple, but generic feel |
| French pronunciation | 6/10 | "Flash" is fine, but sounds English-first |
| International appeal | 8/10 | Works in English, French, German, Spanish |
| Domain availability | 5/10 | flashpark.com likely taken, flashpark.fr more realistic |
| App Store searchability | 6/10 | "Flash" is overused (Flashscore, Flashcard, etc.) |
| Emotional resonance | 7/10 | "Flash" = speed/lightning, good for urgency |
| Conversation test | 7/10 | "J'ai trouvé ma place sur Flashpark" — works OK |
| **TOTAL** | **6.9/10** | Solid but not exceptional |

**Pros:** Clear, describes the product (fast parking), lightning bolt branding works, easy to spell.
**Cons:** Sounds like a utility, not a movement. "Flash" is overused in tech. May blend in with other parking apps.

---

### 10 Alternative Names

#### 1. **Parko** — 9/10
- Short, punchy, friendly, easy in French and English
- "J'ai trouvé sur Parko" — sounds natural
- parko.fr likely available, parko.app very likely
- Feels modern like Vinted, Blablacar, Doctolib
- Mobile-first feel, easy to type
- **Recommended**

#### 2. **Spotr** — 8/10
- "Spot" = parking spot + discovery
- Trendy dropped-vowel naming (Flickr, Tumblr)
- "J'ai spotté ma place sur Spotr" — catchy
- spotr.fr likely available
- Concern: might feel too Silicon Valley for French market

#### 3. **Garely** — 7/10
- From "se garer" (to park in French)
- 100% French-rooted, feels local
- garely.fr likely available
- "Je me gare avec Garely" — natural
- Concern: doesn't work internationally

#### 4. **ParkNow** — 5/10
- Already exists (BMW/Daimler company)
- **Eliminated**

#### 5. **Vroom Park** — 6/10
- Playful, memorable, automotive
- But sounds childish for a marketplace
- Two words = harder for domains and hashtags

#### 6. **Placy** — 7.5/10
- From "place" (parking spot in French)
- Short, cute, app-friendly
- placy.fr likely available
- "Trouve ta place sur Placy" — perfect French
- Concern: could be confused with a real estate app

#### 7. **Stalli** — 6/10
- From "stall" (parking bay)
- Unusual, memorable
- Doesn't resonate in French

#### 8. **ParkFlash** — 6/10
- Reversed version of Flashpark
- Puts "Park" first (clearer intent)
- But same issues as Flashpark

#### 9. **Gareo** — 7/10
- From "se garer" + "geo" (location)
- Feels tech-forward
- gareo.fr likely available
- "Gare-toi avec Gareo" — works
- Concern: too many parking syllables?

#### 10. **Zpark** — 7.5/10
- Ultra-short, punchy, Gen-Z feel
- zpark.fr likely available
- "J'ai réservé sur Zpark" — quick
- The "Z" gives it energy (like Zenpark competitor, though)
- Concern: too close to Zenpark

---

### Final Recommendation

**Keep Flashpark** OR rebrand to **Parko**.

Parko wins on: simplicity, French pronunciation, app-store friendliness, conversation flow, and modern startup vibes (Vinted, Doctolib, Blablacar pattern). It also avoids the "Flash" oversaturation.

If you want to stay international from day one: **Flashpark** is fine.
If you want to dominate the French market first: **Parko** is stronger.

## 2. LOGO CONCEPTS

### Concept A: "The Pin" (Recommended for Flashpark)
- **Icon:** A location pin (map marker) with a **P** cut out of the center. A small lightning bolt accent on the top-right of the pin.
- **Typography:** "flash" in white, "park" in cyan (#06B6D4), Inter Black, tight tracking
- **Small sizes:** Pin icon alone works as favicon
- **Dark bg:** Cyan pin, white text
- **Light bg:** Dark pin, cyan "park" text
- **What it says:** "We help you find your parking spot, fast"

### Concept B: "The Bolt P"
- **Icon:** The letter "P" with a lightning bolt integrated into the vertical stroke
- **Typography:** Bold geometric sans, the P-icon leads the wordmark
- **Small sizes:** Just the bolt-P
- **What it says:** "Instant parking" — the bolt = speed

### Concept C: "The Spot" (Better for Parko)
- **Icon:** A circle (representing a parking spot from aerial view) with a subtle car silhouette inside
- **Typography:** "Parko" in rounded sans-serif, all lowercase
- **Small sizes:** Circle with dot inside
- **What it says:** "Your spot, simplified"

### Concept D: "The Gate"
- **Icon:** An abstract barrier/gate lifting upward, forming an arrow pointing up
- **Typography:** Clean sans-serif next to the gate icon
- **Small sizes:** Simplified gate arrow
- **What it says:** "Access granted" — ties to the QR code / Smart Gate feature

### Concept E: "The Wave P"
- **Icon:** Letter P with a radar/wifi wave emanating from it (like "finding" a spot)
- **Typography:** Modern sans, P integrated as first letter
- **Small sizes:** P with wave arcs
- **What it says:** "Discover parking around you"

**Recommendation:** Concept A (The Pin) for Flashpark, Concept C (The Spot) for Parko.

### 3.2 Launch Strategy (Summer 2026)

#### Why Summer in Nice is Perfect
- **Population doubles** with tourists (2M+ visitors June-September)
- **Parking is HELL** — Nice center, Promenade des Anglais, Vieux-Nice
- **Events:** Nice Jazz Festival (July), Ironman Nice, Carnival leftovers
- **Airport traffic:** Nice is France's #2 airport (14M passengers/year)
- **Beach season:** Everyone drives to the Baie des Anges

#### First 100 Hosts Acquisition
1. **Door-to-door in parking-rich neighborhoods:** Cimiez, Libération, Musiciens — lots of private garages
2. **Facebook groups:** "Nice Entraide", "Vide ton garage Nice"
3. **Leboncoin strategy:** Find people renting garages on Leboncoin → approach them
4. **Revenue pitch:** "Votre place vide = 300€/mois. On gère tout."
5. **Zero commission for first 3 months** → remove friction
6. **Referral:** Host invites host = both get 1 month zero commission

#### First 1000 Drivers Acquisition
1. **Pain-point targeting:** Flyers on cars with parking tickets → "La prochaine fois, Flashpark"
2. **Google Ads:** "parking Nice centre", "parking Vieux Nice", "parking Promenade"
3. **Promo code:** NICE2026 = première réservation gratuite (up to €10)
4. **Partnerships:** Hotels give guests a Flashpark promo card at check-in
5. **Social proof:** Instagram stories from early users → "J'ai trouvé en 30 secondes"

#### Pricing Strategy (Based on Real Scraped Data — 146 Nice listings)

Competitor average in Nice: **€4.04/h | €23.87/day | €141.84/month**

| Service | Nice Competitor Avg | Flashpark Target | Discount |
|---------|-------------------|-----------------|----------|
| Hourly | €4.04/h | **€2.50/h** | -38% |
| Daily | €23.87 | **€15.00** | -37% |
| Monthly | €141.84 | **€90.00** | -37% |

- **Platform commission:** 20% (host gets 80%)
- **No subscription** for drivers — pay per use
- **Dynamic pricing** in summer/events: up to €4/h during Jazz Festival, Ironman
- **Garage spots** can charge premium: competitor avg is €4.29/h vs €3.73/h underground

**Revenue model at 50 active spots:**
- 50 spots × 2 bookings/day × 3h avg × €2.50/h = **€750/day gross**
- Platform take (20%) = **€150/day = €4,500/month MRR**
- Break-even for solo founder: ~20-30 active spots

### 3.4 Competitive Positioning

#### vs Zenpark
- Zenpark = corporate parking lots, monthly subscriptions
- Flashpark = **peer-to-peer**, hourly/daily, instant booking
- **Message:** "Zenpark loue des parkings. Flashpark connecte des voisins."

#### vs Yespark
- Yespark = long-term subscriptions (monthly/yearly)
- Flashpark = **flexible** — book 1 hour or 1 month
- **Message:** "Pas d'engagement. Réservez à l'heure."

#### vs Indigo (ex-Vinci Park)
- Indigo = massive infrastructure, expensive
- Flashpark = **40% cheaper**, same convenience
- **Message:** "Même simplicité. 40% moins cher."

#### Key Differentiators
1. **P2P model** — not corporate lots, real people's driveways and garages
2. **Hourly booking** — competitors mostly do monthly
3. **QR code access** — no keys, no badges
4. **Smart Gate** — automatic barrier opening
5. **Revenue for hosts** — turn dead space into income

#### Taglines (French)
1. **"Arrêtez de chercher. Réservez."** (current — strong, keep it)
2. "Votre place vous attend." ("Your spot is waiting")
3. "Le parking entre voisins." ("Parking between neighbors")
4. "Garez-vous en un flash." ("Park in a flash")
5. "Moins cher. Plus proche. Sans stress."
6. "Le parking réinventé."

#### Value Propositions

**For Drivers:**
- Save up to 40% vs public parking
- Find a spot in 60 seconds
- QR code = instant access
- No subscription, pay per use
- Spots closer to your destination

**For Hosts:**
- Earn up to €1,000/month from your empty spot
- You control your availability
- Insurance included
- Flashpark handles everything (payments, disputes)
- Free to list
